Pittsburgh Forensics Institute

The Pittsburgh Forensics Institute is a low-cost, high-quality summer camp for the Greater Pittsburgh Forensics Community. In a span of two weeks, participants will be able to explore a deep and thorough curriculum for their events modeled after some of the premier camps in the country, with guidance from the most successful members of the North Allegheny Forensics Team.

Who is this for?

Middle School and High School students are encouraged to enroll.

When

August 1st-August 12th, 2016 8:30AM-5:30PM 

Where

Cathedral of Learning, University of Pittburgh

Our Mission

Our motto is simple: High quality for a low expense. With a mix of large group activities and breakout style small group discussions, the PFI curriculum is focused upon the development of higher level analytical and oratorical skills that are essential for success in forensics, and in life. Whether you’re a novice who’s just joined the team, or an experienced speaker wanting to hone in on skills, PFI’s emphasis on holistic learning lets you build on your strengths and patch up the weak spots, Our dedicated and experienced staff has brought home multiple accolades and have regularly beaten out other top competitors; with their help, you too can learn to clinch that trophy and conquer that elimination round. All it takes is two weeks to make your wildest forensics dreams a reality.

What we do

After a successful launch year, PFI is back! The Pittsbrugh Forensics Institute will continue to serve the Greater Pittsburgh Area. Providing quality coaches and experienced students, our one goal is to provide a learning experience in forensics to students who may not have the time to attend camps across the country. If you are interested in speech and debate and want a true learning experience over the summer, PFI is the place for you!
